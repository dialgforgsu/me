About

The Man.
The Moment.
The Mayhem.

G-Su Paek is an actor and improvisor whose presence commands any room — whether it's a packed theater, a film set, or a stage lit by nothing but a single spotlight and pure instinct.

With a background spanning long-form improv, sketch comedy, and dramatic performance, G-Su brings a fearless blend of vulnerability, wit, and raw energy to every role. His work lives in the space where truth meets absurdity — and that's exactly where the best stuff happens.

Based in [City], G-Su performs regularly with top improv troupes and continues to build a body of work on screen that is as unpredictable as it is compelling.
